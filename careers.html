Where Does This Degree Take You?

The tech industry is desperate for people who actually know how hardware and software communicate. Here are just a few paths you can take after graduation:

Cybersecurity Analyst

The Job: You are the digital bodyguard. You monitor network traffic for anomalies, investigate breaches, and hunt down vulnerabilities before attackers find them.

Network Engineer

The Job: You build the internet. You design, implement, and maintain the complex web of routers, switches, and fiber optics that allow massive organizations to communicate globally.

Cloud / Virtualization Administrator

The Job: You manage the invisible data centers. Companies are moving away from physical servers and into virtual environments. You control the massive hypervisors that host thousands of virtual machines at once.
