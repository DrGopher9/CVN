Choose Your Class: Your Future in Tech

Every business, hospital, bank, and gaming studio on earth relies on IT professionals to keep their lights on. Here is what your future could look like right here in Minnesota.

The tech industry is desperate for people who actually know how hardware and software communicate. With a CVN degree, you don't just get a job—you step into a high-paying, fast-paced career. Choose your path below.

Penetration Tester

AKA: The Ethical Hacker / "Red Team"

The Mission: You get paid to legally break into companies. Organizations hire you to hack their apps, bypass their security guards, and crack their passwords to show them where their weaknesses are before the bad guys find them.

MN Avg Salary: ~$130,000/yr

Network Engineer

AKA: The Infrastructure Builder

The Mission: You build the internet. You are the architect behind the massive webs of fiber optics, routers, and switches. Whether you are guaranteeing zero-ping for a massive Esports tournament or keeping a hospital online, you control the traffic.

MN Avg Salary: ~$110,000/yr

Cybersecurity Analyst

AKA: The Digital Defender / "Blue Team"

The Mission: You are the shield. You monitor live network traffic, hunt down malicious hackers who are actively trying to breach your systems, and lock them out. It's a high-stakes game of digital cat-and-mouse.

MN Avg Salary: ~$104,000/yr

Cloud Administrator

AKA: The Virtual Master

The Mission: You build and manage the invisible data centers. Companies don't keep giant servers in their closets anymore; everything lives in the "Cloud." You spin up the massive virtual environments that host things like Netflix, Xbox Live, and global bank databases.

MN Avg Salary: ~$115,000/yr

How Do You Get There?

You don't need a four-year degree to start making real money in tech. At Alexandria Tech, you get hands-on with enterprise equipment on day one. You earn industry certifications while you are in school, meaning you graduate ready to immediately step into the field.
