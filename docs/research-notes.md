## Audience Behavior Signal (Teen UX)
- Pew (published Dec 12, 2024): among U.S. teens, YouTube and TikTok are highly used daily platforms.
- Implication for product direction: short-loop, visual, interactive content is likely to outperform long static pages for this audience.

## Learning Design Evidence
- Meta-analysis (Educational Psychology Review, 2021) reports positive effects of gamification on learning outcomes (`ES = 0.667`) and motivation (`ES = 0.539`).
- Implication: challenge loops, feedback, and progression systems are not just aesthetic; they support measurable engagement and learning outcomes.
