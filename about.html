The CVN Program

Cybersecurity, Virtualization, and Networking at Alexandria Technical and Community College.

Why CVN at Alexandria Tech?

We don't just read about technology; we build it, break it, and secure it. The CVN program is designed around 100% hands-on learning with enterprise-grade equipment.

The Three Pillars

  • Cybersecurity: Learn offensive and defensive tactics. We teach you how hackers think so you can lock down systems before they strike.
  • Virtualization: Master the cloud. You will learn how to spin up, manage, and secure massive virtual data centers.
  • Networking: Get certified. We utilize hands-on Cisco curriculum to teach you how to route traffic across the globe with zero latency.

Beyond the Classroom

Your skills get put to the test in real-world environments. Our students compete in the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC), where they actively defend corporate networks against live, professional hackers. Plus, our high-performance networking principles tie directly into the Alexandria Tech Esports infrastructure.
